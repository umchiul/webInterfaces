Our Product

In developing markets and regions, which are predominantly cash based, regular credit/debit cards are not widely used by customers to make purchases at retail points-of-sale. This is greatly due to the fact that, merchants in these regions are reluctant to pay relatively high transaction fees for conventional debit and credit payment solutions such as VISA and MasterCard. Also, credit and debit cards have the disadvantage of being likely to be misplaced or stolen. Hence, a need for an alternative payment solution that significantly minimizes the merchant’s cost. The end-product of this project is an alternative to current card-based retail payment solutions that leverages Peer-to-Peer Near Field Communication (NFC) technology to facilitate cashless retail sales. This design eliminates the need for conventional debit and credit cards, and their associated risks, and is likely to be accepted in developing regions, as subscribed customers will only need to have an NFC capable mobile phone at hand to make a retail purchase.
