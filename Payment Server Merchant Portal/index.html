About Us

The objective of this project is to design a system consisting of both hardware and software components/devices which will allow subscribed customers to make secure payments to a merchant at a retail point-of-sale using their NFC capable mobile phone. The final goal is to develop a product that will be potentially acceptable in developing markets as an alternative solution to conventional debit and credit card-based retail payment solutions.
